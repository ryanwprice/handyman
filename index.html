What I don't do

I am not an electrican, plumber, or licensed contractor, so I cannot legally do any electrical, plumbing (not including basic faucet installation, etc.) or any structural work.

If you need help with a project or repair and you're not sure whether I can help, feel free to ask. I can always say ‘no’ if it is something I can't do and refer you to a qualified person who can!

About

Ryan Price is your Handyman in the Hammer.

Ryan has years of experience solving problems, fixing things, and making. He is an artist and designer who just so happens to know a thing or two about home repair and construction.

Your home is a special place, and that needs to be respected by anyone working inside it. Ryan strives to maintain a safe & clean work environment and disrupt your environment as little as possible.

The Handyman in the Hammer workspace is an inclusive safe space. Your home is where Ryan works and you can expect Ryan to respect you and your home.

Ryan is insured so you can rest easy when he is working in your home or business.
